Our Creative Showcase

Welcome to our portfolio page! Here, you'll find some of the standout projects created by Steve Keri, the CEO & founder of Websolutely.

Smash and Dash

Smash and Dash is a dynamic, high-performance demo website designed for restaurants. This site features mouth-watering visuals and an easy-to-navigate menu. It captures the essence of the dining experience with high-resolution images that entice visitors and a seamless user interface that makes browsing the menu effortless. Plus, with integrated online ordering and reservation systems, customers can easily engage with the restaurant, driving both traffic and sales.

Features:

  • Stunning food photography showcasing menu items
  • User-friendly navigation and menu browsing
  • Mobile-optimized design for on-the-go access

Bambi Salon

Bambi Salon is a sleek, stylish demo website for a trendy salon. It stands out with vibrant images that capture the salon’s atmosphere and easy-to-use booking integration. This site reflects the modern, chic aesthetic of the salon, giving potential clients a glimpse into the experience they can expect.

Features:

  • High-quality imagery showcasing the salon’s interior and services
  • Responsive design for seamless viewing on any device
  • Stylish and modern layout to attract the target clientele
