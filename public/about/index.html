Meet The Founder

Meet The
founder
Steve Keri

Hi, I’m Steve Keri, the founder and senior website developer at Websolutely. I’m based in West LA and started Websolutely with a simple mission: to build high-quality websites for local small businesses.

In an era where face-to-face interactions and genuine connections are becoming increasingly rare, I wanted to offer small business owners a personal and creative collaborative workflow. My goal is to provide an alternative to the impersonal, rigid, and costly experience often associated with corporate agencies.

At Websolutely, it’s all about real people working together. We focus on building mutually beneficial and synergistic relationships that bring businesses and business owners together, fostering a sense of community and collaboration that helps everyone succeed. Let’s work together to create a thriving online presence for your business.
